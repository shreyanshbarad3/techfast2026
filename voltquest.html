Electrical Engineering Department • First 2 hours event

VoltQuest

3 Round Technical Quiz

Event Overview

Test your technical knowledge, speed, and problem-solving skills in an exciting 3-Round Quiz Competition. Questions will cover basic concepts of Electrical, Electronics, and Mechanical Engineering.

Event Duration & Participation

  • Total Duration: 2 Hours (100 Mins Competition, 20 Mins Results/Judging)
  • Participation: Individual (or Teams as decided by organizers)

Competition Format

  • Round 1 (Preliminary): Basic multiple choice / short answer questions.
  • Round 2 (Technical Challenge): Moderate level conceptual and rapid-fire questions.
  • Round 3 (Final): Advanced buzzer / problem-solving / surprise questions.

Rules & Judging Criteria

  • No mobile phones, smart devices, books, or notes allowed.
  • Negative marking may be applied. Tie-breaker questions will be asked if needed.
  • Judging Basis: Correct Answers, Speed of Response, Technical Knowledge, Accuracy, Overall Score.
