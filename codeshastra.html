CodeShastra

Website / AI Project Competition

Event Overview

Showcase your coding skills, innovation, and technical creativity. Participants will develop a Website, AI Based Application, Smart Software Solution, or Utility Tool based on a given topic and present it before the judges on the event day.

Event Duration & Participation

  • Total Duration: 2 Hours (100 Mins Testing & Presentation, 20 Mins Judging)
  • Participation: Individual / Team (as decided by organizers)

Competition Format

  • Topic will be announced 7 days before the event.
  • Project must be ready before the event day.
  • On the event day, participants must present and demonstrate the working project before judges.

Rules & Judging Criteria

  • Internet can be used during development, but final presentation must clearly explain your own work.
  • Participants must bring their own laptop. Working demo is compulsory.
  • Judging Basis: Innovation, Relevance, Functionality, UI/Design, Coding Logic, Presentation Skills.
