CodeShastra

Coding Challenge Competition

Event Overview

CodeShastra is a competitive coding event where teams work on a real-world problem statement provided after the registration deadline. Participants will develop their solution from their own workspace and submit it before the evaluation day. Shortlisted teams will present their solution before judges.

Event Duration & Participation

  • Registration Start: 21/04/2026
  • Registration Deadline: 27/04/2026
  • Submission Deadline: 30/04/2026
  • Participation: Minimum 2 students and Maximum 5 students per team.
  • Cross-branch Teams: Allowed

Competition Format

  • Problem statement will be availabe here on 22 april 2026.
  • Teams can develop the project remotely from their own workspace.
  • Final shortlisted teams must demonstrate and explain their project on evaluation day.
  • No changes will be allowed after submission deadline.

Rules & Judging Criteria

  • Teams can use any programming language, tools, or frameworks.
  • Use of external resources and pre-written code is allowed, but must be properly understood and explained.
  • Similar solutions across teams will be reviewed carefully.
  • Judging Basis: Problem Understanding, Code Quality, Functionality, Innovation, Presentation & Behavior.
