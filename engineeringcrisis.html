Mega Event • Phase 2 (Next 2 Hours)

Engineering Crisis

Mixed Branch Problem Solving Event

Event Overview

A unique interdisciplinary challenge where participants from different branches come together to solve real-world engineering scenarios. The final output can be presented as a Poster, Non-Working Model, or Visual Presentation.

Event Duration & Team Formation

  • Total Duration: 2 Hours (100 Mins Preparation, 20 Mins Judging)
  • Participation: Individual Registration. Random 4-member teams formed at the venue (mix of IT, Civil, Mech, Elect, etc.).

Competition Format

  • Each team receives a mixed engineering crisis scenario.
  • Teams may use internet and AI tools for research.
  • Final solution must be presented via poster or concept model.

Rules & Judging Criteria

  • Individual registration only; team cooperation is mandatory after random allocation.
  • Working model is optional; clear concept explanation is required.
  • Judging Basis: Teamwork, Practical Feasibility, Creativity, Concept Explanation.
