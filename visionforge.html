VisionForge

Domain Concept Model Challenge

Event Overview

Show your creativity and problem-solving skills by developing a concept-based non-working model on a given domain (e.g. Defence, Agriculture, Smart City, Healthcare, Space Tech). Participants must design an innovative solution and explain its practical use.

Event Duration & Participation

  • Total Duration: 2 Hours (100 Mins Model Making, 20 Mins Judging)
  • Participation: Team Participation (max 4 members)

Materials

  • Provided by Organizers: Cardboard Sheets.
  • To Bring: Glue, Tape, Scissors, Colors, Marker, Decorative/Craft items.

Rules & Judging Criteria

  • Pre-made models are not allowed. Entire model must be prepared during event time.
  • Judging Basis: Innovation & Creativity, Relevance to Domain, Practical Concept, Cleanliness & Finishing, Presentation Skills.
