SkyRise

Newspaper Tower Strength Test

Event Overview

Test your structural design, creativity, and engineering skills by constructing a strong tower using newspapers. Participants must design a stable tower that can withstand load testing.

Event Duration & Participation

  • Total Duration: 2 Hours (100 Mins Construction, 20 Mins Load Testing/Judging)
  • Participation: Team Participation (max 4 members)

Materials & Dimensions

  • Provided by Organizers: Newspapers.
  • To Bring: Glue, Tape, Scissors, Scale / Measuring Tape, Pencil.
  • Tower Dimensions: Base Dimension: 30 cm × 30 cm | Top Dimension: 10 cm × 10 cm

Rules & Judging Criteria

  • Tower must be made primarily using newspapers and must satisfy the required dimensions.
  • Judges will conduct load testing after completion. Tower should remain stable.
  • Judging Basis: Max Load Bearing Capacity, Resistance to Buckling, Accurate Dimensions, Structural Stability, Creativity.
