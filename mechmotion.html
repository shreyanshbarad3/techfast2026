Mechanical Engineering Department • From 10:30 am to 12:30 pm.

MechMotion

Hydraulic & Leverage Models

Event Overview

Show your creativity and mechanical engineering skills by designing a working hydraulic or leverage principle-based model (e.g. JCB Bucket, Hydraulic Crane, Robotic Arm, Catapult, Seesaw, Lever Lift). The model must demonstrate motion, lifting, pushing, or movement.

Event Duration & Participation

  • Total Duration: 2 Hours (100 Mins Construction, 20 Mins Judging)
  • Participation: Team (maximum 4 members)

Materials & Specifications

  • Provided by Organizers: Syringes, Plastic Tubes, Cardboard Sheets, Ice Cream Sticks.
  • To Bring: Glue, Scissors, Tape, Scale, Colors, Rubber Bands, etc.
  • Base working area: 40 cm × 40 cm.
  • Height Limit: Min 25 cm | Max 40 cm

Rules & Judging Criteria

  • Pre-made models are not allowed. Model must demonstrate at least one functional movement.
  • Judging Basis: Working Mechanism Efficiency, Use of Principle, Innovation, Finishing, Smooth Functionality.
