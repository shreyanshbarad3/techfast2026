BuildScape

3D Residential Building Challenge

Event Overview

Showcase your creativity, planning skills, and engineering sense by designing and constructing a 3D Residential Building Model within the given time limit. Participants must build an attractive, innovative, and well-finished residential structure using the provided base material.

Event Duration & Participation

  • Total Duration: 2 Hours (100 Mins Construction, 20 Mins Judging)
  • Participation: Team (maximum 4 members)

Materials & Specifications

  • Provided by Organizers: Cardboard sheet for base platform (40 cm × 40 cm).
  • To Bring: Colors, Scissors, Glue, Pencil, Tape, Scale, Decorative items.
  • Height Limit: Min 25 cm | Max 40 cm
  • Requirement: Create at least a G+1 Residential Building Structure (Ground + 1 Upper Floor).

Rules & Judging Criteria

  • Only provided base platform must be used. Pre-made structures not allowed.
  • Judging Basis: Cleanliness & Finishing, Best Elevation Design, Creativity, Overall Presentation, Proper Use of Space & Dimensions.
